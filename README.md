# Loan-Calculator
##### This app calculates the Monthly Payment, Total Payment and Total Interest on Loans;

### Input:   
###          - Amount
###          - Interest rate
###          - Years to repay
### Output: 
###          - Monthly Payments
###          - Total Payments
###          - Total Interest

### Tools: HTML, Bootstrap4, vanilla JS.
